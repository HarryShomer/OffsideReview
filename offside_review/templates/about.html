About

OffsideReview is a resource for National Hockey League (NHL) statistics. As of now, the data is from the 2016-2017 season until the present for all Regular Season and Playoff games (Preseason is not included). There are currently three views: Skaters, Team, and Goalies. In each view you query data by a variety of different parameters. The option also exists to download your query in the form of a CSV file. The site is updated every morning at 5 a.m. EST.

For those interested in the construction of the site, I use the Django Web Framework (written in Python) for the site. The frontend is comprised of Bootstrap and JQuery. All code can also be found here. The raw data used to create the statistics for this site is courtesy of the National Hockey League API and website. More info on the exact sources of the data and how it's scraped can be found here.

If you wish to contact me to report a bug or suggest a new feature you can get in touch with me either on twitter at @offsides_review or you can email me at OffsideReviewHockey@gmail.com.
